Become a member

The Wellington Botanical Society is always looking to grow—just like the native plants that we study and care about. As a member, you'll be able to take part in field trips to interesting locations around the Wellington region and around the country.


About membership


The membership year for Wellington Botanical Society is 1 July to 30 June. Dues received after 1 May will be credited the following year.

Rates

For new members, the membership rates are:


  • Ordinary members $35
  • Country members $30
  • Groups/families $40
  • Students $10

Application form

You can download the application form here (pdf) or here (Word doc). A member application form is also included at the end of every newsletter.


How to pay

Internet banking

Bank account details for internet banking payment are included on the membership application form.
