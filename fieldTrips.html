Field trips

Day trips to locations in the Wellington region are usually held on the first Saturday of each month. Extended excursions are usually held at New Year, Easter, and on the first weekend in February.


About our field trips


If you want to take part in a Saturday trip, please contact the trip leader by Thursday evening at the latest. If you'd like to join an over-night trip, please tell the trip leader by Wednesday evening at the latest.

This gives the leader enough time to contact our hosts, if we are visiting a private area, to confirm the visit. And it allows means the organiser can print enough copies of a plant species list, if one has been prepared for the site.
