Wellington Botanical Society


Te Ropu Manaki i Nga Tamariki a Tane, o Te Whanganui a Tara

The Wellington Botanical Society encourages the study of botany, with a special focus on native New Zealand flora. Our members work to cultivate native plants and advocate for the protection of lands and waters in their natural state.

Meetings

On the third Monday every month, we get together to see guest speakers, hear about field trips, and chat about botanical topics.

Newsletter

We produce three newsletters a year. This is where you’ll find local botanical news, field reports, and everything else we do.

Join

The Wellington Botanical Society membership year runs from 1 July to 30 June. New members are always welcome!

Identify Plants

Found an unusual plant that you’d like to identify? Maybe we can help!

Field Trips

To observe native plants in their natural environment, we hold day trips to locations in the Wellington region.

More

Find out about Maori plant names, publications, awards, and more!
